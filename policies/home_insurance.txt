HOMEOWNER'S INSURANCE POLICY - HI-2025-001
Policyholder: Amit Verma | Policy Period: Jan 1 2025 - Jan 1 2026

COVERAGE LIMITS:
- Building / Structure Coverage: ₹75,00,000
- Personal Property / Contents: ₹15,00,000
- Liability Protection: ₹10,00,000
- Medical Payments: ₹50,000
- Loss of Use / Additional Living Expenses: ₹5,00,000
- Deductible: ₹10,000

COVERED PERILS:
Fire, lightning, explosion, aircraft damage, riot, strike, malicious damage,
storm, cyclone, flood, inundation, earthquake, landslide, theft, burglary,
falling objects, accidental water discharge, pipe leakage, electrical damage
from power surges.

EXCLUSIONS:
1. Flood damage unless specifically covered under policy add-on
2. Earthquake damage unless included or added separately
3. Mold, fungus, or dampness damage due to poor maintenance
4. Termite, pest infestation, or insect damage
5. Normal wear and tear or gradual deterioration
6. Home-based business equipment beyond policy limits
7. Intentional damage caused by policyholder
8. Nuclear risks, war, or terrorism-related losses unless covered
9. Government action, confiscation, or demolition orders
10. Property left vacant or unoccupied for extended periods without insurer notification

CLAIMS PROCESS:
Report claims within 48 hours. An authorized surveyor will inspect the property within 7-10 business days.
Reinstatement value applies to building/structure coverage. Actual cash value applies to personal belongings unless additional coverage is purchased.
